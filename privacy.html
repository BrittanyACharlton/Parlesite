Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 25, 2026 · Last updated: April 25, 2026

Parle Technologies ("we," "our," or "us") operates the parlé mobile application and website at joinparle.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

By using parlé, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and any profile information you provide. Account authentication is handled by Clerk, a third-party identity provider.

Voice recordings. parlé records your voice during sessions. These recordings are processed to analyze filler words, pacing, and speech patterns. Recordings are stored securely and associated with your account.

Usage data. We collect information about how you interact with the app, including sessions completed, scores, streak data, and in-app behavior. This helps us improve the product and personalize your experience.

Device information. We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system version, and app version for diagnostic and analytics purposes.

Payment information. Subscription payments are processed by Apple through In-App Purchase. We do not directly collect or store your payment card information. We receive confirmation of subscription status from Apple and RevenueCat.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the parlé app and its features
  • Analyze your voice recordings to generate your parlé Score, filler word counts, and coaching feedback
  • Track your progress, streak, and session history
  • Send you notifications related to your training and account
  • Process subscription management through RevenueCat and Apple
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Artificial Intelligence and Third-Party Processing

parlé uses artificial intelligence to analyze your voice recordings and provide coaching feedback. We work with the following third-party AI services:

  • Anthropic (Claude API). Your voice transcripts are sent to Anthropic's API for analysis and feedback generation. Anthropic processes this data in accordance with their privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • OpenAI (Whisper API). Your voice recordings are transcribed using OpenAI's Whisper API. OpenAI processes this data in accordance with their privacy policy at openai.com/privacy.
  • ElevenLabs. Coaching voice audio is generated using ElevenLabs text-to-speech services. ElevenLabs processes data in accordance with their privacy policy at elevenlabs.io/privacy.

By using parlé, you consent to your voice recordings and transcripts being processed by these third-party AI services for the purpose of delivering coaching and analysis features.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Voice recordings are stored for the duration of your account. You may delete your recordings at any time through the app settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers. We share data with third-party providers who help us operate the app, including Clerk (authentication), Supabase (database), RevenueCat (subscriptions), Anthropic, OpenAI, and ElevenLabs (AI processing).
  • Legal requirements. We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfers. If Parle Technologies is acquired or merges with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access. You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction. You may request that we correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion. You may request deletion of your account and associated data through the app settings or by contacting us.
  • Portability. You may request your data in a portable format.
  • Withdrawal of consent. You may withdraw consent to AI processing of your voice recordings by discontinuing use of coaching features.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@joinparle.com.

8. Children's Privacy

parlé is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

9. Canadian Privacy Law

As a company based in Ontario, Canada, we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Ontario's privacy laws. We collect, use, and disclose personal information only for purposes that a reasonable person would consider appropriate in the circumstances.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy in the app or by email. Your continued use of parlé after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
