How It Works
01
Speak. Record.
5 minutes. A prompt. You talk. No script, no performance. parlé captures everything — pace, filler rate, vocabulary, delivery. The truth about how you actually sound.
"14 ums in 90 seconds. Let's work on that."
02
Analyse. Improve.
Your parlé Score gives you a number. Your transcript gets rewritten with stronger words. Your weaknesses are named, not softened. This is what it feels like to have a coach who's actually honest.

Repeat. Transform.
21-day streak. The science of deliberate practice, built into a habit you'll actually keep. Day 21, you'll notice. Day 100, you'll play back your first recording and not recognize her.
